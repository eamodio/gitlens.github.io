About GitLens

GitLens started way back in mid-2016, when I fell in love with Visual Studio Code and wanted to play with the then newly released extension support. It was also a good excuse for me to improve my budding TypeScript skills. It all started with a simple question — could I add Git insights via code lens to any document.

Today, GitLens has over 7 million installs and ~50M downloads 😱! It is loved and relied upon by millions of users, on teams of all sizes, ranging from single developers to large enterprise teams. GitLens has quickly grown from a simple prototype into a feature-rich product with a mission of its own — to simply help you better understand code.
